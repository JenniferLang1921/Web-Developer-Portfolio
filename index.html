Jennifer Lang

Web Developer and Designer

Hello, my name is Jennifer Lang. I create simple, beautiful, and functional websites. When I'm not coding, you'll find me painting, reading a book, or exploring the great outdoors.

I decided to be a web developer because I thought I would get the chance to be creative, do research, and learn new things continually. I used to be a music teacher, but decided against it because I like creating on my own more than I like being in charge of large groups of people. I took some time off after teaching to raise my kids, and had a part-time job doing online marketing for a real estate company. My boss was a computer science major and trained me in how to do online marketing, and from there I began to explore the internet. I’ve had many blogs, and discovered I liked designing them just as much as writing them. Building and creating web pages is exciting for me. I love that technology is always changing so there will always be something new for me to learn.
